What is new?

In its 5th iteration since its inception, Alphanode 2019 is poised to be drastically bigger and better than it has ever been before. We have reimagined cryptic hunts with decodenode quests: a new storyline-based game mode. We are also introducing RoboCTRL, a robotics event with a fresh challenge, new robots and exciting battles every year.
